About Us

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology (CUET), established in 1984, is the pioneer of computer science and engineering education in Bangladesh. As the first department of its kind in the country, CSE CUET has consistently set the standard for excellence in education, research, and innovation, and has been widely recognized on domestic and global stages. Since its establishment, the department has been attracting the best and the brightest students of the country. The department offers Bachelor's, Master's, and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science and Engineering, and regularly updates the curriculum to maintain international standards. It is also offering specialized postgraduate programs (Master's and Ph.D.) in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI & ML), Data Science, Cyber Security, Software Engineering, and Computing to nurture the next generation of leaders and innovators in these rapidly evolving fields. The recent expansion in this front is supported by the Ministry of Education (Bangladesh) and funded by the Asian Development Bank (ADB). The world-class education and research activities here are designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and mindset needed to thrive in today's and tomorrow’s dynamic tech landscape. With a highly competitive environment, state-of-the-art facilities, and a team of dedicated faculty members and staff, it is ensured that students reach their full potential and emerge as trailblazers in their chosen field. Currently, the department hosts about 700 undergraduate students and 400 graduate students, all of whom are driven by a shared passion for excellence and a commitment to making a difference in the world. The students have participated and secured commendable positions in the International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC) world finals regularly in the last three decades, and became Asia West Champions in 2021 and 2023. The research outcomes of undergraduate and post-graduate students are regularly published in prestigious journals and conferences, and students have received numerous awards including many best paper awards. Students of the department regularly get admitted to top ranked universities including MIT, UC Berkeley, CMU, etc., for postgraduate degrees, which is a testament to the excellence of the students and the quality of education at the department.
